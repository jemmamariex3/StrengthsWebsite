ABOUT

Every person has a unique set of strengths that can lead them to achieve confidence, success, and a sense of purpose.

 

In a world that focuses on overcoming weaknesses, few people are aware of their strengths, which can be cultivated in order to overcome shortcomings, rather than dwell on them.

Through discovering and building your strengths, you can grow into your most authentic, successful self.

 

Achieving academic and career confidence that will ensure bold decisions, purposeful goal setting, and a solid sense of validation and self-worth is at the heart of discovering your strengths.

While each person has their own unique set of Strengths, it can be useful to understand where each of them came from.

 

The 34 Strengths themes are divided into four themes, which are:

Strategic Thinking, Influencing , Relationship Building, and Executing.

STRENGTHS

THIRTY-FOUR

34 Strengths were created to help everyone, from students to professionals, discover and describe their own unique assets within an easily understood context.

 

By making a unique, and functional, language of Strengths, we can communicate our successes and further our own development.

Knowing your Strengths will allow you to maximize your abilities in all aspects of your life, from the classroom to the office. The CSUN Career Center will help you with tips to apply your strengths to decision making, academic and career goals, and even extracurricular activities.
