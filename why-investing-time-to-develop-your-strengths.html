WHY INVESTING TIME

Successful people are those who work hard, maintain determination, act with passion, and uphold integrity. They endeavor to always be moving forward, improving both personally and professionally.

 

The quest for personal excellence is a daily practice amongst successful people, and should be at the forefront of your mind when approaching any situation. Striving to be your personal best will culminate in the realization of your amazing potential.

 

Our talents hold the key to greatness, so working to build up your Strengths is an important part of personal excellence. Focusing on one Strength at a time, and aiming to understand and develop that Strength, is the best route to excellence.

 

In this way, you can give each of your Strengths the attention they need to become clear in your mind and to flourish.

 

When you put the time and effort into enhancing what is already strong within you, you magnify and raise your most talented and unique self, creating a climate ripe for success and achievement.

 

Taking the time to gain the knowledge and skills that complement and increase your Strengths will give you confidence, ambition, and follow through in all areas of your life.

 

Using your Strengths to choose the best classes and extracurricular activities on campus can enhance your academic experience extraordinarily!

 

You will find that exemplary grades come more naturally, and that you have a sense of purpose within your academic career.

 

But developing your Strengths toward this end will require practice, hard work, and commitment to your self-improvement. With preparation and persistence, you can achieve excellence!

 

Thriving in your work environment can come down to knowing where your Strengths can be best utilized to assure smooth and successful operations within your company.

 

There are specific tasks that you will be particularly good at, and identifying these and taking initiative will manifest an extremely fulfilling career.

 

You’ll be poised and primed for interviews, important tasks, problem-solving, and leadership opportunities, because you will know yourself and what you have to offer.

 

 

It is important to remember that your five unique Strengths, in their unique order, truly make you the individual that you are.

 

The chances of having the exact same Top 5 Strengths in the exact order as someone else is 1 in 33.39 million! The chances of having the same Top 5 Strengths as someone else, in any order, is approximately 1 in 340,000.

 

These numbers are proof that only you can bring your particular gifts to the table. Only you can apply those particular five Strengths together and absolutely shine!
