APPLY YOUR STRENGTHS

34 STRENGTHS

34 Strengths were created to help everyone, from students to professionals, discover and describe their own unique assets within an easily understood context.

 

By making a unique, and functional, language of Strengths, we can communicate our successes and further our own development.

 

 

Knowing your Strengths will allow you to maximize your abilities in all aspects of your life, from the classroom to the office, from working with teams and building relationships to maximizing abilities and excelling at any given task, the Career Center will help you with tips to apply your strengths to decision making, academic and career goals, and even extracurricular activities.

 

Start applying your Strengths to become your most successful self!
