FOUR DOMAINS

While each person has their own unique set of Strengths, it can be useful to understand where each of them came from. The 34 Strengths themes are divided into four categories, which are: Relationship Building, Executing, Strategic Thinking, and Influencing.

 

While it would be ideal to have every strength represented in a group, more realistically having someone from each of the four Strengths Domains goes a long way into ensuring all bases are covered.

 

By knowing whether you fall into Relationship Building, Executing, Strategic Thinking, and Influencing, you can see where you’ll fit best in a team dynamic.

 

Many people fall into more than one Domain, but don't worry if you don't. Let’s explore each of them to understand them better:

EXECUTING

Like the director of a play or an impassioned public speaker, those with Influencing Strengths empower everyone around them. Influencing individuals bring ideas to fruition and inspire others to push forward.

 

Whether it is in a small team or a global company, Influencers don’t often think small. Instead, they put their energies into reaching a larger audience.

 

Look to those with Influencing Strengths when you need advice on expanding a business or promoting a non-profit

RELATIONSHIP BUILDING

If information analysis and creative problem solving are areas in which you excel, you might have Strengths in the domain of Strategic Thinking.

 

While others may get bogged down by the moving parts of any project, a Strategic Thinker can sort through a constant stream of information and see a path forward with clarity.

 

They provide a team the forward planning and focus necessary to make wise decisions. Those with Strengths in Strategic Thinking challenge the status quo, inspiring others to think bigger and broader, in order to make the most of each idea presented to them.

When someone is strong in the Relationship Building domain, they are sure to consider the human element in every situation when solving problems.

 

In a team, these people-oriented individuals are the glue that holds everyone together, allowing the group to become greater than the sum of their parts.

 

Those with Relationship Building can inspire others to see connections between people and ideas, creating harmonious and collaborative environments.

Hard working and reliable, the Executing domain possesses Strengths that strive for success.

 

On teams and for themselves, executing individuals are tenacious, with the ability to generate to focus in on their goals and to generate energy for their accomplishments.

 

Comfortable both behind the scenes and in the spotlight, executing individuals will put forth the effort to solve any new problem and make sure that preparations are made ahead of time. This domain flourishes in positions of authority and they lead by example, practicing what they preach with gusto.
